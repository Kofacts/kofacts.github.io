introducing avae v1.0

January 2026 · 2 min

The ONLY assistant that actually finishes the work.

Decisions happen once. Follow-through happens every day.

You approve a hire, sign a contract, or kick off a project. You think it’s handled. It isn’t. The next step depends on an email, then a reply, then an update in a system you don’t check. A week passes. You realize the work never actually happened.

No one forgot on purpose. The process just dissolved into the silence.

We have tools to track work and tools to talk about work. But the responsibility for finishing remains trapped in your head. You have to remember to chase. You have to notice the stall. You are the glue holding it all together.

We built Avae to take that burden off you.

Avae doesn’t suggest plans or send reminders. It owns the outcome. When you define an objective, Avae coordinates the steps across your tools and waits for real-world signals; a cleared payment, a filed document, a merged pull request, to verify the work is done.

If a process stalls, the system doesn’t just sit there. It responds. It retries. It reroutes. It only pulls you back in when the situation requires human judgment.

Execution stops being something you manage in your head. It becomes something the system finishes.

We’re opening a small, private beta with operators who are done chasing work across tools and people.

This is software that finishes work, literally.
